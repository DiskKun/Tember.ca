What is His Personalized Copy?

His Personalized Copy is a ROM hack of the infamous Super Mario 64, designed and based off of the popular "every copy of Super Mario 64 is personalized" theory/conspiracy/rumour! Explore entirely new "personalized" versions of the classic levels you've come to know and love. Created with love by Jefftastic and the HPC Team.
